Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2025

Reel Log ("we," "our," or "us") is a fishing log app that lets you track catches, locations, and fishing sessions. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose, your name and email address.
  • Catch and trip data: Fish species, size, weight, notes, dates, and any other details you enter when logging a catch or session.
  • Location data: If you grant permission, we record the GPS coordinates of your catches and fishing spots. This data is stored in your account and is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Purchase information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your App Store transaction data to verify your subscription status. We do not store your payment details.
  • Usage data: Firebase may collect anonymous crash reports and analytics to help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To verify your subscription status
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. Firebase data centers are located in the United States. By using Reel Log you consent to your data being processed there.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and cloud database — Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Handled by Apple — Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with the service providers listed above, as necessary to operate the app.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Reel Log is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at jscrossing@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at jscrossing@gmail.com.
